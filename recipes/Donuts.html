Donuts without the glaze

Description

This recipe is perfect for anyone starving for donuts without the sweet part (deafeating the purpose of donuts).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 a cups of brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of mill
  • 1/4 cup of butter which is softened

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit or 165 degrees celsius
  2. Make donuts: Stir flour, brown sugar, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, salt, and baking soda altogether in a large bowl until they are mixed well.
  3. Bake in the oven for about 8 to 10 minutes
  4. Place the donuts onto the baking sheet to cool
